Welcome to The Hunt.
An online cryptic hunt for those who like their minds to be challenged.
The Hunt is an online cryptic hunt, where players crack ciphers, codes and puzzles - clues for which are all over the web. Fire up your inner sleuth and get ready to go down the rabbit hole once again. All means are allowed and encouraged. The Hunt will not only require your knowledge but also reasoning and resourcefulness on the internet.
The Rules:


  1. Use the clues and riddles scattered in the pages to find the answer to the levels
  2. You shall need to make the most use of the internet to help you find the solutions to the levels
  3. The format for answering is replacing the levelname in the URL with the solution you procured
  4. This is a strictly single-player game. If at any point players are suspected of cheating via interaction, they shall be removed from the game
  5. The first player to complete all levels wins. Ranks are also determined on the time spent in the level, but not the total time
  6. May the best player win!
